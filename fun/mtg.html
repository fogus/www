I'm a long-time MtG player (from the beginning) who for most of my time has enjoyed constructed play over every other modes of play. From the beginning I've mostly enjoyed tempo and/or control decks as I find that they provide the most opportunity for depth of play, even when they're not the strongest cards in whichever format I find myself. I prefer to play the same decks over and over rather than change in order to gain a deep understanding of the patterns that the deck presents.
